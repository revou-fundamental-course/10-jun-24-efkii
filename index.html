How to convert form Celsius(°C) to Fanrenheit(°F)

The temperature° in degrees Fahrenheit(°F)is the same as the temperature° in degrees Celsius(°C) times 9/5 plus 32

S(°F) = (S(°C) x 9/5) + 32
